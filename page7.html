Internship Program

Internship Requirements:
» Malaysian students who are currently pursuing either Diploma or Bachelor’s Degree programs at public or private universities in any related field.
» Only applicable to university students who are currently in their 2nd year or above.
» Strong command of English and Bahasa Malaysia (written and spoken).
» Have excellent written and verbal communication skills.
» Strong attention to detail and organizational skills.
» All applicants must submit a cover letter and resume.
» Able to commit to an attachment period of 8 weeks and above.
